Who We Are

Ennov8 Design Group, LLC (Ennov8) is proud to be a locally owned and operated Engineering Consulting company with a strong focus on Transportation Design. We are conveniently located in Central Florida and provide professional services throughout the State. Ennov8 is small sustainability business enterprise (SSBE), minority business enterprise (MBE) and disadvantaged business enterprise (DBE). Ennov8 was established based on the following core values:

  • Embrace challenges
  • Inspire innovation
  • Empower trust & integrity
  • Be proactive
  • Teamwork focus
  • Elevate quality
  • Be visionary
  • Build globally, serve locally
